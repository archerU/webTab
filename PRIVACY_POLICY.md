# Privacy Policy for webTab

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

webTab ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

webTab stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Shortcuts and Categories**: Website shortcuts, custom categories, and their organization
- **Settings**: User preferences including background images, language settings, and display preferences
- **Custom Icons**: Icons you upload or select for your shortcuts

### Data Synchronized to Your Google Account

If you are logged into Chrome with your Google account, the following data may be synchronized across your devices:

- Categories and shortcuts
- Settings and preferences

This synchronization uses Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.sync` API, which is managed entirely by Google and subject to Google's privacy policy.

## How We Use Your Information

- **Local Storage**: All data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage
- **Synchronization**: Data is synchronized to your Google account only if you are logged into Chrome, using Chrome's native sync functionality
- **No External Servers**: We do not transmit, collect, or store your data on any external servers
- **No Analytics**: We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, or tracking information

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with any third parties.**

- Your data remains on your device or in your Google account
- We do not sell, rent, or trade your information
- We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services
- We do not send data to external servers

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using browser storage APIs
- Synchronized data is encrypted by Chrome's sync service
- We do not have access to your synchronized data stored in your Google account
- No data is transmitted over the internet except through Chrome's built-in sync (if enabled)

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access**: You can view all your data through the extension's settings
- **Export**: You can export your configuration data at any time
- **Delete**: You can delete categories, shortcuts, or all data through the extension's settings
- **Control Sync**: You can disable Chrome sync in your browser settings to prevent data synchronization

## Data Retention

- Your data is stored locally on your device until you delete it
- If you uninstall the extension, local data may be retained by your browser
- Synchronized data in your Google account will be retained according to Google's data retention policies
- You can delete all data at any time through the extension's settings

## Children's Privacy

webTab is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the extension's description on the Chrome Web Store

## Third-Party Services

webTab may use the following third-party services:

- **Google Favicon Service**: To automatically fetch website icons (domain names only, no personal data)
- **Chrome Storage API**: For local storage and optional synchronization (managed by Google)
